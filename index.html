    • Robb Rockmoninoff, who is known to most as simply "Robb Rock", began his music career at an early age. Hailing from Atlanta,GA, he has always been a fan, student and proponent of good music. He has a strong musical background which is deeply rooted in performance. Robb Rock got his start as a member of his Elementary school band and continued his passion throughout his secondary matriculation where he acquired his skills in reading, playing, writing, and composing music. While attending Morehouse College, he was able to strengthen his network being amongst the future of the thriving Atlanta music scene.

    He has studied under world-renowned musical instructors including Ken Roberts, Melvin Maurice Miller, Melvin Jones, Edward Emory, and Reginald Colbert. Though rooted in performance, Robb Rock is no stranger to the music business, as he has witnessed, first hand, the success of his uncle, multi-platinum artist, and Grammy award winner, Peabo Bryson, for whom Robb currently serves as road manager. His uncle has been his music mentor since he was first introduced to the craft.

    Robb Rock's talents and knowledge in production, engineering, and music as a whole have gotten him to where he is now, and his music has reached the masses and gained the attention of many in the music industry, both indie and major. Look to see and hear his name even more in the near future.
